# 📊 Power BI Sales Analysis Project

A comprehensive sales analysis dashboard built with Power BI, providing actionable insights across sales performance, payment methods, shopping mall trends, and customer segments.

## 📈 Project Overview

This report analyzes retail sales data across multiple shopping malls in Nigeria, uncovering key trends in customer behavior, payment preferences, and product categories.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🗓️ Overview Insights
- **Strongest Sales Day:** Monday leads in sales while Sunday is the weakest
- **Average Transaction Size:** ₦169,000 per transaction — indicating high-value or bulk purchases
- **Top Locations:** Enugu and Onitsha are the hottest sales cities, ideal targets for future growth

### 💳 Payment Method Insights
- **Cash** remains the dominant payment method across all genders, malls, and high spenders
- **Credit Cards** are popular especially in high-value transactions and among female customers
- **Roban Gift Cards** are the least used — opportunity for promotion and incentivization
- **Agbani Mall** and **Trans-Ekulu Mall** lead in total sales, driven by cash and credit card payments

### 🏬 Shopping Mall Insights
- **Fashion** drives the majority of sales across all malls
- Top performing malls: **Agbani**, **Trans-Ekulu**, and **Independence Layout**
- Lower-performing malls could expand Fashion offerings to boost revenue

### 👗 Category Insights
- **Fashion** is the #1 revenue driver and key focal point for business strategy
- **Balanced Customer Segments:** Both high spenders and bargain shoppers contribute significantly, with a larger base of bargain shoppers
- **Gender:** Balanced spending pattern across genders in average sales and category breakdowns
- **Growth Opportunities:** Bakery and Household categories need increased marketing and product diversification

### 🎯 Personalized Marketing Strategies

**For High Spenders:**
- Introduce exclusive deals on premium products
- Give early access to new and premium inventory
- Loyalty rewards and VIP programs

**For Bargain Shoppers:**
- Focus on discount campaigns and coupons
- Bundle offers that appeal to price-sensitive customers
- Promo sales and seasonal discounts
